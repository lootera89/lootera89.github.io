Demono: Home Alone - Lootera89

Unreal Engine Atmospheric Design Solo Project

Demono: Home Alone is a short, story-driven horror game I developed solo. It gained significant popularity on its launch, ranking high in itch.io's most popular section. The story, written by two friends and voiced by a professional artist, while I did all of the design and development.

This game focuses on creating an immersive atmosphere using my custom PSX style dithering effects and layered sound design. with its detailed visuals and interactive elements, the game is highly optimized with a final size of just 400 MB.

  • Dithering post process material for PSX aesthetics
  • Many player to world interactive objects
  • Scalable quest system
  • Level streaming and HLODs for optimization
  • Layered audio, real time cutscenes & voice acting

3 Days

Volleyball With A Stranger - Lootera89

Unreal Engine Quick Development Solo Project

Volleyball With A Stranger is a short horror game I developed and designed solo for Secret Santa Game Jam in just 3 days. In this game players face a stranger in a tense volleyball match with life or death scenario. the story has 2 potential endings.

The game focuses on dynamic AI behavior and escalating tension through atmospheric design. It features smooth gameplay mechanics, eerie sound effects, and a minimalist approach to storytelling.

  • Predictive AI for enemy volleyball shots
  • Curve based physics calculation for ball shots
  • Implemented AI to dynamically track and follow the ball
  • Optimized gameplay with final 200MB game size
  • Sound / UI / world / narrative design, and PSX visuals
