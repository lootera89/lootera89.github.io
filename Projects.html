Rgames - Fabwelt Studio

Unreal Engine Game Development

In April 2023, I joined Fabwelt Studios to develop a racing game as their first Unreal Engine developer. Known for blockchain FPS games like Arsenal, Fabwelt aimed to expand into different genres. The project involved creating an AAA-level racing game MVP to help secure funding.

For six months, I worked solo, tackling challenges in AI, realistic car physics, and UI elements to bring this game to life. From dynamic car AI to advanced motion blur, the game was designed for realistic, immersive experiences even on low-end PCs.

  • AI development for opponent cars
  • Advanced minimap, speedometer & UI/UX
  • Custom motion blur and visual effects
  • Realistic car physics and car controls
  • Niagra, Level streaming, World design, etc.
