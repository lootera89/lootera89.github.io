I'm Kushagra, I've been a gamer since I was 6. my passion for game development began In 2017. I learned to program in C++ in 2021 and started developing games with Unreal Engine 4 the following year. After that, I moved to Unreal Engine 5. I have also worked with SDL3, Godot, Unity, Renpy, and other game engines.

I've worked on several projects you can check them out in the projects section. About a year ago, I worked with Yellow Whale Labs on an open world multiplayer game called Hidden Beyond. Before that, I was with Fabwelt Studios, where I worked on a AA racing game, Rgames. Two years ago, I worked on a indie Godot game at a small studio called ITE.

In my free time, I worked on personal projects under my studio, Lootera89. I’ve shipped a couple of my indie games: Demono: Home Alone, Fistful of Gore, and Volleyball with a Stranger. For both Mobile and PC. Recently, I joined the Secret Santa Jam 2024 and made a game in just 3 days. It became one of the top submissions!
