MAKING OF

In 2022, I started working on a cool indie game idea. It was a strategy game where you build an army of rats to fight against clever cats. You'd clear levels, earn and spend money, and upgrade your rat army.

I got pretty far with the game, but then exams came up, and I had to put it on hold because of work. I've been busy with work ever since, so I haven't had the chance to finish it yet. But I'm definitely going to complete it someday!

In the game, I created a quite creative start menu. It is the HQ of your rat army, where you could see a rat hole, a market, an atlas, and tasks button. Each button had a different job. I also made a bunker where you could see all your rats in the army and a place where you could hire new rats. Plus, I added an inventory system so you could customize your boss rat. These were some of the cool things I did in the game, and I'm excited to finish it when I get the chance.
